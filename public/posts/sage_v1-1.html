What is SAGE?

SAGE (Storyboard Animation Generation Environment) is an open-source multimedia AI orchestration platform. It functions as a command center, merging creative generation, development assistance, and intelligent orchestration under one cohesive system.

"Open-source multimedia AI orchestration platform for creative storytelling — runs fully on Android via Termux."

SAGE acts as the manager of your workflow. It uses PHP and bash automation to control external inference engines for rapid generation.

The platform distinguishes itself with a visual-first, responsive user interface featuring a mobile-friendly multi-gallery system, allowing for intuitive swipe navigation and asset organization on touch devices.

Under the hood, SAGE relies on a rich semantic database designed to represent every aspect of a story, effectively functioning as a dedicated movie-storyboard visualization engine.

This architecture enables creators to manage complex narratives and distinct story elements within a sequential timeline while orchestrating batch workflows and handling storyboards locally, with heavy processing seamlessly delegated to remote servers.
